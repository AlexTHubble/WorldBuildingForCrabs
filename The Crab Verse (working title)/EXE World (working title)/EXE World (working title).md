## Important setting information
**(name of world once decided)** is backdropped by a major tragedy that shook the world causing it to currently be in a state of recovery. Known as [[The Joy plague]] it caused a supernatural depression to rip through the world, causing the world to atrophy for it's 30 year duration. At it's peak, the sorrow was so strong that nearly every inhabitant was in a state of severe depression, it only ended once [[The EXE bomb]] was detonated. This purged the plague and allowed humanity to start to rebuild, at the cost of draining nearly all of the [[EXE]] from a large portion of the continent creating the [[Null desert]]. 

The world has been rebuilding for the past 20 or so years, however the disruption has weakened the central governments. While it is not a place of chaos and warlords, the country is in a state similar to the wild west. Gangs have a strong foothold and most towns govern themselves. 

[[Empaths]] have a large role to play within the climate of the recovering world, Minor Empaths with relevant abilities tend to play major parts. Major Empaths are extremely rare, they tend to be treated more as folk tales. Empaths reputation will vary from location to location, some believe that they caused [[The Joy plague]] while others believe that without them it never would have been stopped. In reality neither of these are true, but the superstition still spreads like wildfire.  

Many country borders have shifted and deteriorated as people find their place in this new world, in the wake of the first generation post plague (known as [[The Generation of Regrowth]]) coming to adulthood the world is in a state of fluctuation. This generation often clashes with [[The Generation of Sorrows]] due to ideological differences. The older generation craves stability and stasis, where the newer generation craves expression and change. 
### Technology
Technology in **NAME OF WORLD** is entirely analog based, [[EXE]] based tech was commonplace before [[The Joy plague]] but has become taboo to a large portion of the populace due to fear of a second plague. An unknown side-effect of [[EXE]] on this world is the disruption of radio waves and electronic information storage, this has caused technology to remain largely [analog](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Analogue_electronics).
