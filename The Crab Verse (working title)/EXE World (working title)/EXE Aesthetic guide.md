Much of the vibe of this world comes from the [Shibuya Punk](https://aesthetics.fandom.com/wiki/Shibuya_Punk) aesthetic design. The new generation using vibrant art and technology clashing against the depression era old aesthetic. Drawing from [Hiphop](https://aesthetics.fandom.com/wiki/Hip-Hop), [Skater](https://aesthetics.fandom.com/wiki/Skater), [punkrock](https://aesthetics.fandom.com/wiki/Punk), [Midwest Emo](https://aesthetics.fandom.com/wiki/Midwest_Emo), and [graffiti pop](https://aesthetics.fandom.com/wiki/Graffiti_Pop).

Major population centers are in a phase of regrowth after becoming abandoned after the plague, despite still having a haunting emptiness. 

Small towns / cities dot the landscape, becoming the destination for people fleeing the cities. 

Most people cannot afford personal transportation, making public transit and small scale transit (bikes, skating, ect) a must.
