EXE (short for Experience Energy) is the energy produced by human experience, it's properties are largely defined by the specifics of the experience that created it. For example a traumatic experience will create a sorrow and/or pain EXE, a happy experience will produce joy EXE, ect. Humans by nature absorb most of the EXE they produce, however when a particularly strong response is had to stimuli the excess EXE is leaked into the atmosphere causing [[Echo(s)]]. 

Researchers have been able to categorize these into 10 pairs of EXE (explored by American psychologist [Robert Plutchick](https://www.6seconds.org/2025/02/06/plutchik-wheel-emotions/) in 1980), each type will help determine both how it manifests as [[Echo(s)]] and how [[Empaths]] are able to channel them
