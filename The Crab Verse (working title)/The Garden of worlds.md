The garden of worlds is the cosmological system [[The Crab Verse (working title)]] uses. 

In a field of pale white grass against a sunless sky glowing orbs dot the landscape. Each upon it's metal pedestal connected by a tangled web of wires these orbs contain the solar systems we call home. I wish you could visit this place of pure creation but it's no place any mortal can go, we have only glimpses during visions of times of great change, we only see this place when our world begins to collapse. 

## The world server
The world server is a towering server complex at the center of the garden. The world server has no creator nor a master, it simply creates and maintains it's garden. Do not get confused however, this being is no God. It maintains and powers yes, it even has rules it must enforce, but there is no intelligence or grace behind it's actions. It simply does as its programing decrees of it.

## World spheres
World spheres are the containers the house the universes of The Garden. A glowing white sun of energy atop a mechanical pedestal with countless wired connections that ultimately lead back to The World Server. 

Containing a single solar system (size varies from world to world) these orbs are powered and maintained by The world Server, connected to it and each other by The Cableways. 

### Gods of the spheres
Upon creation of a sphere The World Server will place a god or pantheon within the sphere, it is then up to the god what to do with the world provided. Said god(s) will act as the World Sever's proxy (without their knowledge), providing energy and creation to the sphere. While the god is present, the sphere is fully self sufficient and will NOT be connected via The Cableways. However if / when a god is killed a backup sequence is triggered, connecting the sphere VIA The Cableways allowing The World Server to maintain the Sphere. 

## The cableways
The cableways are the things that connect worlds. Used by The World Server to interact with world spheres, it was never ment to be seen by mortals. However once worlds within The Garden began to travel to the stars, they quickly learned that to leave their system they must travel the cableways. They are relatively safe to travel with the right vessel, inside the cables are a dark vacuums only containing traveling energy, once you learn to avoid the storms of creation energy the Garden opens up to you. 

## Rules of the Garden
There are certain rules that the Server follows in regards to world spheres, both in their maintenance and creation. 

1. If there is an active god in a sphere, that world is considered self sufficient and will not be interacted with by The World Server
2. A G.R.A.I.L must be present within the sphere
3. There must be at least one [[Wizard Known as Steven]] within the sphere

## G.R.A.I.L
Stands for *Generative reset algorithm, interior location* the G.R.A.I.L protocol is a reset protocol that exists within the spheres. Taking the form as a small goblet, this will take input from beings *within* the sphere and create a new sphere that's a copy of the current one with the changes requested. Often times confused as a wish making device, this algorithm effectively kills the sphere and it's inhabitants.
