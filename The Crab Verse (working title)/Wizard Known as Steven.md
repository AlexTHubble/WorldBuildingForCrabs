The Wizard Known as Steven is an entity that exists within every world sphere created within [[The Garden of worlds]]. It's unknown exactly why The World Server creates these wizards, or why once a world is connected via the Cableways the world's Steven becomes mentally connected to every other Steven in the Garden, but we know it's a rule. 

The Stevens are known to meddle in affairs claiming to be benevolent. To some they're a highly respected group of philanthropists, to others they're meddling assholes poking their noses in business they have no right to be in.  

## The System known as Steven
Once connected the Stevens of the Garden joined together in a desolate sphere they renamed to *The System known as Steven*. Rebuilding the system for their needs it's become a hub for trade and politics within the Garden.

## The Steven Link
The Steven Link is the phenomenon attributed to connecting all the Stevens together. Importantly this is not a hive mind or psychic connection, rather it's an awareness and shared knowledge. If you've met a Steven you've been introduced to every Steven, despite every individual having their own memories.
