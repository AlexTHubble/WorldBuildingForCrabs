EXE (short for Experience Energy) is the energy produced by human experience, it's properties are largely defined by the specifics of the experience that created it. For example a traumatic experience will create a traumatic EXE, a happy experience will produce happy EXE, ect. Humans by nature absorb most of the EXE they produce, however when a particularly strong response is had to stimuli the excess EXE is leaked into [[The Inbetween]]

Researchers have been able to categorize these into 10 classifications (explored by American psychologist [Robert Plutchick](https://www.6seconds.org/2025/02/06/plutchik-wheel-emotions/) in 1980), each type will help determine both how it defines in [[The Inbetween]] and how [[Empaths]] are able to channel them. 

### Pairs of EXE classifications
*Note: the majority of these are self explanatory, so only important observations are mentioned*
- Joy // Sorrow
	- Joy - One of the most Common EXE's produced however simultaneously the most rare within [[The Inbetween]], this is largely due to Humans naturally wanting to hold as much as possible, rarely letting go.
	- Sorrow - Common in both generation and within [[The Inbetween]], Humans tend to let go of excess sadness quickly leading to a large buildup.
