## Campaign Tone & setting
- Campaign takes place in the city of Duskvol, a haunted Victorian style city where gangs run rampant
- The party will be playing a cult dedicated to [[The  Shepherd of Souls]], a mysterious being dedicated to the ghosts of the city. 
	- While the intents are mostly unknown, the party interprets the goal as noble, helping the ghosts of the city
- Campaign tone is serious with room for goofs & shenanigans
