Old Humanity named this system and it’s planets after their literature, having recovered the flight paths the Second Comity used these names in their colonization of the system. It was only recently that the original seed ship was discovered. This ship was filled with as much literature, fiction and nonfiction, as old humanity could fit. Unfortunately there were zero survivors, and all electronics fried beyond repair, so the original intent is a mystery to this day.

In the modern times, Shakespeare is an unstable system trying to grow into a Utopia. Union with the help of the Tolkien government and the mega-station Silver-stein is building a gate that they hope will pressure the remaining governments into adopting the Utopia standards. If this will succeed without violent confrontation? Only time will tell.
