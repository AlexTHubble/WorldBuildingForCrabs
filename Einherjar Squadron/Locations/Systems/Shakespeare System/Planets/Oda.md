## Pirate world, within [[Shakespeare System]]
During initial colonization projects Oda was off the table due being an ocean world. As interstellar law enforcement grew to match piracy rates, outlaws were in desperate need of a world to hide out on. Shortly after the discovery that Oda’s water structure blocks long ranged detection methods, it became a prime location for outlaws to hide out.

Most outlaws prefer deep water mobile stations, however there are a few that found an alternative home on Oda. The massive migrating rock formations, held afloat by a dense ecosystem of water based plant life, roam the seas of Oda in regular patterns. These are used as mainly export hubs, allowing external space ships to land and depart without specialized deep sea equipment.
