## Cocoon world, within [[Shakespeare System]]
## Project Cocoon
After witnessing what can happen when outside ideas infect a civilian population on [[Rowling]], the shadow government of Orwell was born. Paranoid of ideas they started the encasing of their world, convincing their population that Rowling’s fate was due to an outside influence, a literal thought virus, they got the public support of the project.

The exterior of Orwell is a known factor in the system, doubling as both a wall and a giant military base the Cocoon is a symbol of both power and paranoia. The interior of Orwell is another concern, no one outside Orwell knows what goes on within and no one from the inside will tell.

## Shadow Government
Continuing the theme of mysteries, no one knows the identity of the heads of Orwell. Anything about the local leader of a branch is completely anonymous, even to the local branch. It’s unclear how any of this works without interference, but anyone who’s made an attempt has had zero success.

## Resistance
No authoritarian state is absolute, no though barrier impenetrable, so resistance is a given. Based on Orwell’s moon [[Seus]] there is an ongoing effort to smuggle refugees out and supplies in. While Orwell actively fights the resistance, their gorilla tactics has kept them out of the line of fire.

## Interior
All of this information comes from the [[Orwell Resistance Army]], there are no official documents, pictures, or descriptions of the interior of Orwell. The interior is a collection of villages under a giant roof that emulates the sky and weather. With “strands” connecting giant support beams holding up the ceiling, further strengthening the feeling of being within a woven cocoon. There are government bases and cities exist within the pillars and along the strands, with a great highway within the infrastructure enabling transit.
