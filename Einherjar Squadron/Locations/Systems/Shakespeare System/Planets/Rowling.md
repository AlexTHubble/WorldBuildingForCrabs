## Mostly uninhabited, tomb world, part of Shakespeare system
Rowling is currently a tomb world inhabited by refugees and highwaymen. After a large scale rebellion against the former government, the government decided to scorch the remaining of the planet to send a final message. This event had ripples throughout the entirety of [[Shakespeare System]], causing [[Orwell]] to encase their planet out of paranoia of their fate being the same, [[Tolkien]] to rise up and begin their own Utopia project, and the [[Strawhats of Oda]] to establish themselves, helping the refugees in ways a lawful organization couldn’t.
