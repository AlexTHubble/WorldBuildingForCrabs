## Inhabited, free planet, part of the Shakespeare system
Tolkien is a free democracy and currently the leading connection to the rest of the universe. Covered in picturesque fields and forests, citizens live in a comfortable world inching closer to post scarcity.

## Military
Tolkien’s military is both out of necessity and heroism, after seeing the result of the [[Rowling]] rebellion the consensus from both the government and the civilian population is that no other rebellions can be ended with scorched earth from the oppressors. Watching Orwell‘s devolution into an authoritarian information state, Tolkien’s military hopes to free the civilians with the help of Unity once the gate is built.
