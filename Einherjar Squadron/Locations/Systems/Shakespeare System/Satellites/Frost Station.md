## Gateway of the Coccon, satellite of [[Orwell]], within [[Shakespeare System]]
Frost is the only legitimate entrance and exit to Orwell’s interior. Known as both a rich person’s playground and brutal police state Frost is mostly a facade designed to manipulate outsiders into believing in Orwell’s prosperity. Filled with casino’s, brothels, pubs, and any form of cheap entertainment you could want it’s the Vegas of Shakespeare System.

All of that changes when you enter the lower levels, connected to a massive space elevator only government officials are allowed to use it for civilian transport. Other than that It’s mostly used as a freighter, taking goods in and out of the interior of the Cocoon.

Orwell working class on the station are trapped, when coming up they sign away their ability to return and contact the interior of Orwell. While they can legally leave, few make enough to be able to ferry out, and fewer are willing to take the chance ferrying them out in the first place. Workers with families work with a social program to send money back to their family inside, without knowing if said family is even still alive
