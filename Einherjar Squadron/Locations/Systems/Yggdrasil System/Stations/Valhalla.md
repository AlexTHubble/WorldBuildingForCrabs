## Station within [[Yggdrasil System]], location Unknown
A utopic station hidden by [[Huginn and Muninn]]. entrance only granted to a few inhabitants of the system, the criteria unknown. Said to be a paradise where those chosen live the remainder of their days. None have been chosen since the fall of [[Asgard]], and the twin A.I.s decreed nothing about it. This is the chief mystery the [[Vanaheim station]] researchers hope to discover on the ruins of [[Asgard]]
