## Bio
Ozen Engdahl was born into one of the wealthier families of [[Poe]], known for the multitude of factories. His family, as he’d learn growing up, held a rather dark secret. They were always boisterously loud about their disapproval of less well off people inhabiting the planet, even working for them, but he’d learn how deep their hatred went by the age of 10. His father would bring him to a gruesome basement in one of the factories, littered with various body parts and corpses still needing to be dismembered, disdain in his voice as he spoke of the “vermin and leeches” that infested every planet.

By 13, Ozen couldn’t take his parents cruelty, and would kill his parents, leaving behind his baby brother(age 5 at the time). By the time he’d reach 18, Ozen would go on to take down a few dozen other wealthy families, those that particularly held the same cruel world view as his parents. While he’d succeed in assassinating the head of a major medical equipment manufacturer, he’d slip up and get caught, weapon in hand.

6 months into his long prison sentence, the head of Poe military would pay him a visit, offering a reduction in his sentence for doing odd missions for the military, strictly off books, of course. Ozen would agree, demanding in writing the terms and number of missions he’d need to complete before being allowed free.

Now, at 28, Ozen was discharged from both prison and his “military” contract, looking for work where he can find it.

In between jobs, if he’s able, he likes to gamble, often only playing small stakes games for quick easy payouts, but has frequented some larger venues on rare occasion. Barring that, he’s always making adjustments to his rifle.

Given the notoriety behind his early career of waste removal, Ozen would likely be approached about joining the crew.
